The Downtown Diner is a family run restaurant with home style foods and friendly staff. We typically serve staples of cuisine such as hamburgers, french fries, club sandwiches, and other simple and quickly cooked food. Much of the food is grilled, as early diners were based around a gas-fueled flat-top. Coffee is a diner staple.
